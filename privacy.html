Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pearl Loop handles information when you use the mobile game. If you have any questions, contact us at 508923216@qq.com.

Information We Collect

Pearl Loop is designed as an offline puzzle game. Core gameplay progress, level unlocks, star ratings, settings, and best times are stored locally on your device.

The app may request App Tracking Transparency permission. If you allow tracking, advertising identifiers such as IDFA may be used by advertising partners to deliver and measure ads. If you deny tracking, ads may still be shown, but they should not use IDFA for cross-app tracking.

The app may also process technical information such as device model, operating system version, app version, approximate region, ad events, crash or diagnostic information, and security signals needed to operate the app, protect the service, and load advertising configuration.

Advertising and Third-Party Services

Pearl Loop may use third-party advertising and related SDK services, including advertising networks and measurement providers. These services may process information under their own privacy policies. The app template includes integrations for multiple ad platforms and a security SDK. Ads are loaded only when the app receives the needed configuration and an ad is available.

How We Use Information

  • To provide gameplay, save local progress, and remember user settings.
  • To show, load, limit, and measure advertisements.
  • To maintain app security, prevent abuse, and diagnose technical issues.
  • To comply with platform requirements and applicable laws.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited technical or advertising-related information with third-party SDK providers when required for ads, measurement, security, diagnostics, or legal compliance.

Children

Pearl Loop is a general audience puzzle game and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and delete it where required.

Your Choices

You can change tracking permission in iOS Settings. You can also disable music and sound effects inside the game. Deleting the app removes locally stored game progress from your device.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect information processed by the app. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
